Governance Rules
🗳️

Removing any board member requires a 75% supermajority vote (4 of 5 board members).

📜

Amending the bylaws requires a 75% supermajority vote (4 of 5 board members).

🎭

Any vote affecting the Artistic Director's authority, compensation, or role requires an 80% supermajority vote.

🔒

Dissolving the organization requires a unanimous board vote (5 of 5 members must agree).

📅

Board must meet quarterly. Quorum is 3 of 5 members. No meeting may conduct official business without quorum.

📝

Minutes must be kept for every board meeting and retained in official records.

🚫

WARNING: Do NOT solicit donations or accept tuition until the 501(c)(3) application has been submitted -- or until you have consulted with a Michigan nonprofit attorney. Doing so before filing may jeopardize your tax-exempt status.
