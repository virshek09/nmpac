Our Story

The Upper Peninsula has no shortage of talented kids, dedicated families, and people who believe in big things for small towns. What it has been missing is a home for them.

Northern Michigan Performing Arts Center exists to bring world-class performing arts training right here to this region. We are building a place where dancers, actors, singers, and musicians of all ages and backgrounds can train seriously, grow boldly, and belong completely.

We believe in excellence and we believe in this community. Those two things are not in conflict. This is not someday. This is now, and it is happening right here in Ironwood.

NMPAC is rooted in this community and committed to excellence, creativity, and belonging. Every family, every artist, every age. A rising tide lifts all boats, and we are here to build something that lifts everyone.

Our Space
